About SDG 4 — Quality Education

Understanding the United Nations Sustainable Development Goal 4

🎯 What is SDG 4?

SDG 4 aims to ensure inclusive and equitable quality education and promote lifelong learning opportunities for all by 2030.

📊 Key Targets

  • Ensure free primary and secondary education for all
  • Equal access to affordable vocational training
  • Eliminate gender and wealth disparities in education
  • Universal literacy and numeracy

📚 How StudyHub Helps

StudyHub connects learners with free educational resources using the Open Library API — making quality education accessible to everyone, everywhere.
